A tool for analyzing cancer data from The Cancer Genome Atlas

Currently, this tool enables visualization of gene expression (RNA-seq) data from primary solid tumor samples via heatmap and violin plot.


Explore and Define Your Cohort:

1) Select cancer type(s)

2) Select gene(s)

3) Click pie sectors to define gene types

Hint 1: Click items in legend to change view of plot

Hint 2: In plot, click on wild-type or mutated genes that you would like to visualize. Selected data will be highlighted yelllow.

Hint 3: To undo/reset selections of pie sectors, you must refresh the page.
